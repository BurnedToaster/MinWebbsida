Non DLC Subclasses

Void

Suppress: If a combatant is suppressed they will become disoriented

Weaken: The effected target will take increased damage

Volatile: The effected target will explode after taking additional damage

Invisibility: Vanishes from sight and does not appear on radar

Overshield: Gain a additional health bar over your current one, reducing damage

Devour: When getting a kill, automatically heal all lost health

Solar

Radiant: When radiant, deal increased damaged towards enemies

Ignite: When a target ignites they will deal explosive damage to surrounding enemies and them self

Scorch: When a target is effected with scorch they will take damage over time, and after a certain amount of scorch the target will ignite

Cure: The player is healed in a quick burst

Restoration: The player will continuously regenerate health and shield, while not taking damage

Arc

Amplified: When amplified, gain increased movement speed and better weapon handling

Blind: If a combatant is blinded they will be disoriented and unable to fire their weapon

Jolt: IF a combatant is jolted they will chain small lightning bolts to nearby enemies

Stasis

Stasis Crystal: When a stasis crystal is created, nearby combatants or slow nearby players

Stasis Shard: When collecting a stasis shard, gain a small amount of melee energy

Slow: A slowed target will have reduced movement, ability regeneration and reduced weapon performance, and after a certain amount of slow the target will freeze

Freeze: When the target is frozen they will be unable to move and after taking additional damage while frozen, the target will shatter

Shatter: When a target is shattered they will take damage and deal damage to the surrounding area

Child Of The Old Gods

Cast your rift to create a Void Soul. When you damage a target with a weapon, your Void Soul flies to them and drains them, doing damage and weakening them.

Fusion Grenade: Explodes twice. While airborne, rapidly defeating targets with your Super or weapon grants Cure to you.
